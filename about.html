Hi I'm Kathleen and I'm a UX Researcher and Designer!

I'm currently finishing up my degree in UX Research and Design at the University of Michigan.

Before working in UX, I had always been very interested in technology and I graduated from DePauw University with a degree in Computer Science. But while I loved working with code, I quickly realized how passionate I was about designing technology for users and decided to transition to UX.

Since switching careers, I've been able to leverage my skills from my software engineering career and worked on some amazing teams to solve problems for clients like international nonprofit Sahiyo, the Michigan Bureau of Elections, as well as intern with semiconductor company AMD.

I'm someone who loves a challange, so outside of UX, you can find me at CrossFit, learning how to knit, or reading a good book.
